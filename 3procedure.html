Layout design of Digital logic circuits - Logic Gates



Step by Step Procedure


1. Go to the simulator tab and click on any of the link provided. Multiple links may be available on the page corresponding to different sub-experiments and methods of implementation. Once you click on any link, a new web page will pop-up which will form the platform for conducting the virtual experiment.

2. On the new window, provisions are available to change the default input settings (frequency, phase, voltage ...etc) by editing the code section.

3. Click on the simulate button for starting the simulation experiment.

4. Wait until the output waveforms are displayed on the window. This delay depends on the speed of the internet connectivity.

5. Once the output appear, check for its logical validity. Compare it with its theoretical expectation.

6. Repeat the experiment with different set of inputs.
